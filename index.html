Privacy Policy

WordSight is an Ad Supported application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

This application does not collect or store personal data. The application is available on Google Play Store and Google Play Store collects some personal data. Please see the privacy policy for Google Play Services here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions Requested by the Application

On Android the following permissions are requested by the WordsSight application:
Permission Why it is required
INTERNET to fetch the definitions for words.
CAMERA to detect words.
STORAGE to backup and restore the database (used to store details of words learnt everyday). Note: Does not store the definitions of the words.
